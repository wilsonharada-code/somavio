High-priority fine risks

These are common visitor mistakes that can create real problems in Banff National Park, Canmore or Kananaskis.

Fine risk

Recreational drones

Recreational drones are not allowed in Banff National Park. Flying without authorization can lead to enforcement action and fines up to CAD $25,000.

Official source →
Fine risk

Feeding or disturbing wildlife

Do not feed, entice, approach or disturb wildlife. Violations can lead to court and fines up to CAD $25,000.

Official source →
Park access

Park pass check

A valid park pass may be required for Banff National Park outside free-admission periods. Check dates, fees and pass requirements before leaving.

Age-restricted local laws — 18+

This section is for legal-awareness only. SomaVio does not promote alcohol or cannabis and does not provide purchasing guidance.

Restricted local law

Public alcohol or cannabis use

Rules vary by exact place: town, hotel, campground, trail, event area or national park land. Do not assume public use is allowed. Follow posted signs and official local rules.

Safety rule

Driving and public spaces

Never drive after alcohol or cannabis use. Hotels, shuttles, campgrounds and public areas may have stricter rules than visitors expect.

Restaurants, cafés and shops

Local culture

Tipping

Tipping is common in Canadian sit-down restaurants. Counter-service tips are usually optional, even when the payment screen suggests a preset amount.

Local basics

Taxes

Listed prices may not include tax. The amount paid at checkout can be higher than the menu, shelf or service price.

Before you go

Reservations and timing

Popular restaurants can fill early on weekends and holidays. Kitchens in mountain towns may close earlier than visitors expect.

Transport, parking and timed activities

Check first

Parking

Parking can fill early in Banff and Lake Louise. Check official parking or transit options before departure.

Before pickup

Shuttles and tours

Arrive early and confirm pickup point. Mountain transport schedules can be strict during high season.

Before you go

Rentals

Gear and bike rentals may require ID, a credit card or a deposit. Check pickup and return time before arriving.

Wellness and private spaces

Spas, hotel services and private businesses may have quiet-space expectations, photo/phone rules and cancellation policies. SomaVio should show these as gentle microtips after the visitor has clicked the main CTA.
