Peak & Valley Guides

Certified guides · Small groups · Bear safety included

What's good today

First time in the Rockies? No experience needed. Peak & Valley leads small groups of 4–8 on beginner-friendly trails with real wildlife safety training. Every tour includes bear awareness, proper trail behaviour, and how to use bear spray safely. Parks Canada approved curriculum.
